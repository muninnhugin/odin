Vietnamese Chicken Wings

Description

Salty, sweet, and tangy, this dish is the perfect Vietnamese dinner party or family meal plate

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings: 4-5 pieces
  • Garlic: 2 heads
  • Ginger: 1 head
  • Fresh chili: 1 head
  • Cooking wine
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Fish sauce
  • MSG
  • Black pepper
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Mince garlic and ginger
  2. Marinade chicken wings with 1-2 tablespoons of fish sauce, half a tablespoon of pepper, half a tablespoon of MSG, 1 tablespoon of cooking oil and a dab of ginger.
  3. Put a saucepan on low-medium heat with cooking oil. Once the oil is hot, put the wings in one by one. Once they are golden, take the wings out of the saucepan.
  4. Using the same saucepan, saute garlic with 1 tablespoon of fish sauce and 1 tablespoon of sugar until golden. Put the chicken wings back in the saucepan with heat on high. Taste and adjust seasoning as necessary. Once taste is right, put in chili and turn off the heat.
  5. Put the chicken with sauce on a plate and decorate with lettuce, tomato slices, and cilantro.
